SILENYTE Project

The Silenyte project is an undergoing collection of productions derived from a Worldbuilding project. Comic, RPG, Encyclopedia, Music, and much more as far as the world goes. Brought to you by yours truly, Lucas Flicky and cohorts.
Here you can check lively on the production status as I feed the site with info about the scenario.

This site in the future will be primarily in English, bringing the Portuguese translations alongside. But for broader accessibility I'll release consolidated material in English first. Protype material will be released in Portuguese first, e.g. the FAQ below.
